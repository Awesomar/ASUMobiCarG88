ASUMobiCarG88

It is a RC Car project. It allows complete mobile control over the car using bluetooth signal. The car allow "easy driving with obstacles avoidance", "accurate movement" and "line tracking" features. The project is done under the guidance of Dr.Mohamed Sobhy.
